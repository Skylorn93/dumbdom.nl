Contacting Gob

The Gob's communication methods include:

  • Smoke signals (highly ineffective, he's afraid of fire)
  • Carrier pigeon (often mistaken for lunch)
  • Telepathy (but only if you believe hard enough)

If none of these work, just yell really loudly. He probably won't answer, but you'll feel better.

Good luck!
